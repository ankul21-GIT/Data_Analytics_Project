# Power_BI_Project
#  Sales Analytics & Business Intelligence Dashboard

##  Overview

This project is a complete **end-to-end Sales Analytics solution** designed to help businesses understand their sales performance, identify profit-driving products, and make data-driven decisions.

It answers critical business questions such as:

* Which products generate the highest and lowest revenue?
* How do sales trends change over time?
* What is the relationship between sales and profit?
* How do discounts impact profitability?
* Which cities or regions perform best?

The project uses **data analytics, SQL, and visualization tools (Power BI)** to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

---

##  Business Problem

In many organizations, sales data exists but is **not effectively analyzed**. Businesses struggle with:

* Identifying **top and underperforming products**
* Understanding **sales trends over time**
* Measuring the **impact of discounts on profit**
* Comparing **performance between different time periods**
* Lack of centralized dashboards for decision-making

 This leads to **poor strategic decisions**, revenue loss, and inefficient operations.

---

##  Solution

This project provides an **interactive analytics dashboard** that:

* Tracks key sales KPIs
* Identifies top/bottom performing products
* Analyzes sales trends (daily, monthly, yearly)
* Shows relationship between sales and profit
* Enables dynamic filtering (Product, Date, Customer, Category)
* Helps compare performance across different time periods

---

## Key Features

### 1️. Top/Bottom Product Analysis

* Top 5 and Bottom 5 products based on:

  * Sales
  * Profit
  * Quantity Sold

### 2️. Sales Trend Analysis

* Daily, Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly trends
* Helps identify seasonality and growth patterns

### 3️. Sales vs Profit Relationship

* Scatter/visual analysis of revenue vs profitability

### 4️. Time Period Comparison

* Compare performance between:

  * Two months
  * Two years
  * Custom date ranges

### 5️. Discount Analysis

* Average discount across categories
* Impact of discounts on profit

### 6️. Order Insights

* Total number of orders
* Order-level breakdown with filters

### 7️. Interactive Dashboard Filters

* Product
* Date
* Customer ID
* Promotion Category

### 8️. Geographic Analysis

* Sales performance by city
